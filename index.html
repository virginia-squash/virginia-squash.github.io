Mission

Virginia Squash is run by volunteers who love the game. We are here to support players and grow the sport. We run leagues, organize tournaments, host socials, and develop underserved youth into well-rounded athletes.

Squash Rocks

Since 2010, Squash Rocks has been developing underserved youth into well-rounded athletes through the sport of squash. Based at the Anna Julia Cooper School in Richmond, the program serves around 20 kids year-round.
